Frequently Asked Questions

    We are currently only offering general transcription services

  • I have a large project. Can you handle that?

    Yes. We have a large team of experienced transcriber to handle the work

  • Which language do you transcribe?

    Currently we are only handling English and Kiswahili files

  • Which files types do you support?

    We can handle mp3, mp4, Wav, Mov, and Avi

  • What are difficult audios?

    Audios with background noise

    Audios which require frequent timestamps

    Audios with thick accent

    Audios with medical or scientific terminology which require research
